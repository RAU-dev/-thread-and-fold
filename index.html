The Journal — Entry 12

Why we stopped
dyeing past
the root.

Every colour in this run comes from onion skin, madder root, and iron liquor — nothing synthetic touches the cloth. It means shirts fade honestly instead of pilling into grey, and no two batches are ever quite the same shade.
